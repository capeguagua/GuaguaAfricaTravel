🇿🇦 Cape Town City DIY: Table Mountain, Bo-Kaap & Waterfront

1 Day City DIY

About This Journey

Welcome to South Africa's Mother City — the only city with Clean Audit status. From Table Mountain's 360° cable car to the Castle of Good Hope, Bo-Kaap's colorful streets, Kirstenbosch gardens, Constantia vineyards, and the V&A Waterfront. Multiple curated combo plans for every style.

Itinerary

09:00

Castle of Good Hope

South Africa's oldest colonial building. Explore the military museum and learn about Dutch East India Company history.

10:30

Bo-Kaap & Cape Malay Quarter

Stroll through colorful streets, visit the Bo-Kaap Museum, taste Cape Malay cuisine.

12:00

Greenmarket Square & City Bowl Lunch

Browse the flea market for local crafts, then lunch in the City Bowl area.

14:00

V&A Waterfront

Shopping, Zeitz MOCAA museum, Nobel Square, and waterfront dining.

16:30

Table Mountain (cable car or hike)

Take the cableway or hike Platteklip Gorge for panoramic views of Cape Town.
